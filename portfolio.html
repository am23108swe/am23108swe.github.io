Current Research

4D printing of Hydrogels

Study of Design Functionality on Hydrogel Actuator

I conducted a study on determining how the design of the hydrogel hinges (rectangular, hexagonal, triangular) affected the hydrogel actuation.

Using Fusion 360, CAD design of master mold is created.

The master mold is printed using 3D printer.

Silicon mold is prepared using the master mold.

Cured hydrogel from silicon mold is placed in water for controlled time.

Hydrogel actuation is seen after 3 min 30 seconds.

The graph of curvature vs the number of hinges and design of hinge is recorded.
